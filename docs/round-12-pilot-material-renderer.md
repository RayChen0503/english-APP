# Round 12 - Pilot Material Renderer

This round turns the Round 11 pilot material contract into renderable Markdown content.

## Added

- `PilotMaterialsRenderer`
- Teacher brief Markdown renderer
- Consent notice Markdown renderer
- Feedback form Markdown renderer
- Observation sheet Markdown renderer
- Complete material pack renderer

## Why This Matters

Round 11 defined the pilot materials as structured data. Round 12 makes those materials easier to export into documents, Google Docs, forms, or classroom handouts.

## Output Sections

- Teacher Brief
- Consent Notice
- Feedback Form
- Observation Sheet

## Guardrail

Rendered materials keep the same low-pressure principle: no public ranking, no raw score comparison, and no framing that treats emotional breakpoints as failure.
